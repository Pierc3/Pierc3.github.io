The program starts by opening a window, asking if you want to continue or exit. It then moves on to the first question, the second, and so on. The quiz game has ten questions in total, 40 answers in total, 4 to each respective question. You cannot move on to the next question until you guess the current question correctly. After you beat the quiz game, you are given a congratulatory pop up box, and the user is to close the game after that.
